Rules:  

  • Three Strikes Suicide Style Rules

  • Multiple entries allowed by the same person (multiple entry fee required)

  • Each week, you will select one NFL team that you think will win (ties count as a loss).

  • Make your pick each week on the pool pick website.  (See Deadline below).

  • If you fail to submit a pick, or your picked team doesn't win, or your picked team does not play, you will get a strike.

  • You cannot select a team more than once over the course of the season.  If you do, it will count as a "no pick" which results in 8 wins for tie-breaker purposes.

  • If you pick a team that is not playing, it will count as a "no pick" rather than a pick of that team. 

 

Scoring:  

  • Survivor Pool for all NFL Games. Participants will receive a strike for each incorrect pick.  Upon receiving the third strike, you are eliminated.

 

Deadline:

  • The pool pick deadline for each week is Sunday at noon eastern time.  Your pick must be made by that time.  However, if you want to pick a team that is playing earlier than Sunday, you must make your pick prior to kickoff of that game.  The website will lock out all pre-Sunday games at kickoff and will lockout all remaining games (including Monday night games) on Sunday at noon eastern.  There are no exceptions and no overrides.

  • The website allows you to make picks for future weeks at any time.  You can change them subject to the bullet above.

 

Finances:

  • 100 point entry fee.  25 points additional late entry fee if not confirmed by 08/31/2016

  • Pick Website Expenses: $20  (to be paid by the winner)

  •  1st Place: 70% of entry fee pool minus Pick Website Expenses (est. 1,100 points)

  •  2nd Place: 20% of entry fee pool (est. 320 points)

  •  3rd Place: 10% of entry fee pool (est. 160 points)

 

Tie-Breakers:

In the case that more than one player remains at the end of the regular season, a tie-breaker will be used in the following order:  

1) Player with the fewest strikes at the end of the season wins.

2) Player whose picked teams have the fewest number of total wins for the season wins.  Note that a "no pick" week (i.e. a week where you fail to submit a pick or pick a team that is not playing) will count as 8 wins against you for tie-breaker purposes.

3) Player who obtained his last strike latest in the season wins.

4) Player who obtained his first strike latest in the season wins.

5) In the event that there is still a tie after applying all tie-breakers, the prize pool will be split among those still tied.

 

In the event that more than one person is eliminated in the same week, a tie-breaker will be used in the following order:

1) Player whose picked teams have the fewest number of total wins as of the end of the elimination week is considered to have been eliminated later.

2) Player who obtained his second strike latest in the season is considered to have been eliminated later.

3) Player who obtained his first strike latest in the season is considered to have been eliminated later.

4) Player whose picked teams have the fewest number of total wins as of the end of the week prior to elimination is considered to have been eliminated later.

5) In the event that there is still a tie after applying all tie-breakers, the prize pool will be split among those still tied (e.g. if two people tie for 3rd place, they'll get 70 points each).
