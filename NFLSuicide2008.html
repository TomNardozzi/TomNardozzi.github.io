Rules

  • Three Strikes Suicide Style Rules

  • Each week, you will select one NFL team that you think will not lose.

  • Send email to threestrikesnflpool@gmail.com with your pick, at least one hour prior to kickoff of the first game of the week.

  • If you fail to submit a pick, or your picked team loses, or your picked team does not play, you will get a strike.

  • You cannot select a team more than once over the course of the season.

 

Scoring:  

  • Survivor Pool for all NFL Games. Participants will receive a strike for each incorrect pick.  Upon receiving the third strike, you are eliminated.

 

Deadline:

  • Picks must be submitted by one hour before week's first game.  No exceptions.  If no pick is submitted, you get a strike for the week.

 

Finances:

  • $100 entry fee.  Winner(s) take all.

 

Tie-Breakers:

In the case that more than one player remains at the end of the regular season, a tie-breaker will be used in the following order:  

1) Player with the fewest strikes at the end of the season wins.

2) Player whose picked teams have the fewest number of total wins for the season wins.

3) Player who obtained his last strike latest in the season wins.

4) Player who obtained his first strike latest in the season wins.

In the event that there is still a tie after applying all tie-breakers, the prize pool will be split among those still tied.
