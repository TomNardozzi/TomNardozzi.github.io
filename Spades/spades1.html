Forfeit Rule:

A team can forfeit at any time. However, if they don't finish the current hand in progress, the result is recorded as 500 - -200. If they do finish the current hand, the result is 500-0 unless the spread between the scores is > 500. If that's the case, then the result is the actual score at the end of the completed hand that contained the forfeit.

If one player forfeits, but their partner does not, the remaining player will play out the game with a bot to its conclusion or until the remaining player forfeits under the rule stated above. The result of the game will apply to both players.
